Fast setup checklist

  1. Enter the real school-day times. Use the time you leave home for commute, then the official school start and finish.
  2. Select only the days that count as school days. For most schools this is Monday to Friday.
  3. If the school uses Day 1, Day 2, Day 3, turn on the repeating cycle, set the cycle length, and enter the calendar date of Day 1.
  4. Paste cycle subjects in this exact format: Day 1: Maths, English, Science. One day per line.
  5. Press Save school setup. After that, add tutoring, sport, and clubs in Commitments.
